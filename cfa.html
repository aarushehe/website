ðŸ“˜ CFA Program Structure

The CFA Program consists of three levels:

  • Level I: Focuses on foundational knowledge and understanding of investment tools and ethical standards.
  • Level II: Emphasizes the application of concepts through complex case studies and analytical problem-solving.
  • Level III: Integrates all concepts with a focus on portfolio management and applying knowledge to real-world scenarios.

Work Experience Requirement: To earn the CFA charter, candidates must complete at least 4,000 hours of relevant professional work experience, spread over a minimum of 36 months.
