About Explore NPV

At Explore NPV, we empower students and professionals to navigate and build successful career in finance. With over four years of experience, we’ve been dedicated to helping individuals identify their path and take confident steps toward their goals in the financial world.

Our guidance begins as early as high school, giving students a valuable head start in this competitive field. We offer comprehensive support for key finance programs such as CFA, FRM and Financial Modelling. Beyond academic preparation, we also assist candidates with placements—especially freshers.

Our mentorship extends beyond exam preparation, offering continuous support throughout the journey—from learning to launching a career.

Vanshika Agarwal

Founder & Mentor

Working as a finance educator, Vanshika Agarwal founded Explore NPV, where she guide students and professionals in carving out their path in the world of finance. As a CFA Charter-Holder and a Certified Financial Planner (CFP), her journey as a mentor began right after clearing Level I of the CFA program, when she discovered her passion for teaching and helping others navigate their own journey.

Having appeared for competitive MBA entrance exams like CAT, NMAT, and SNAP, she received admission offers from top B-schools such as IIM Kozhikode, NMIMS Mumbai and SIBM Pune. But her passion for Finance boosted her to take the tough decision of rejecting the admission offers from Top MBA Colleges and continue in the field of education.

With these principles in mind, she is continuing to provide her guidance and services to the students and helping them in laying the groundwork for successful career in finance through comprehensive training in programs like CFA, FRM, and Financial Modelling.
